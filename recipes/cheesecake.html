Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Classic cheesecake is easily one of the greatest desserts to ever touch the white draped dinner table.

Silky, creamy, smooth and crunchy all in a single mouthful. Follow this easy homemade recipe to cure that lust for dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Cream Cheese
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 Teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Teaspoons of Lemon Juice
  • 3 Eggs
  • 10 Inch Pie Crust
Caution Use all Ingredients at room temperature or cheesecake batter could become chunky and over-beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F(177°C)

  2. Using a stand mixer, beat the 2 pounds of cream cheese and 1 cup of granulated sugar until the mixture is creamy.

  3. Add the 1 cup of Sour Cream, 1 teaspoon of Vanilla, and 2 teaspoons of Lemon Juice. Mix thoroughly until completely blended.

  4. Set the mixer to medium speed and add the eggs one at at time, adding the next egg after the last has fully blended with the batter.

  5. Pour the cheesecake batter onto the pie crust.

  6. Bake for 55-70 minutes.

  7. Let the cheesecake cool at room tempurature for 1 hour.

  8. Cover and refridgerate for at least 4 to 5 hours.

  9. Lastly, uncover and eat to your stomachs content.

If dropped please let your mom or grandma know so they can help you.

The cheesecake gods will not be pleased with you.
